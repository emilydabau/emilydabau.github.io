About Emily
Emily Dabau is a student journalist who has interned at House Beautiful and Good Housekeeping magazines in New York City as well as the Miami New Times, Broward-Palm Beach New Times and Gulfstream Media Group in South Florida. Her work has also been published online for Cosmopolitan, Seventeen, Redbook, Elle Decor, Marie Claire, Esquire, Veranda, Country Living and MSN.com.

At the University of Miami, Emily studies journalism and public relations. She has worked for the student newspaper, The Miami Hurricane, since her freshman year and has served as copy chief, assistant editor and online news editor. She is currently the arts and entertainment editor.
