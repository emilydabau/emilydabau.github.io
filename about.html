About Emily
Emily Dabau is a student at the University of Miami majoring in journalism and public relations. She has worked for the student newspaper, The Miami Hurricane, since her freshman year and has served as copy chief, assistant editor and online news editor. She is currently the arts and entertainment editor. She has been a Hearst digital media intern, working for House Beautiful and Good Housekeeping magazines, and has also interned at the Miami New Times, Broward-Palm Beach New Times and Gulfstream Media Group. Her work has been syndicated on Cosmopolitan, Esquire, Redbook, Elle Decor, Marie Claire and MSN.com.
